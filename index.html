How it works

Frequently Asked Questions

A commission is a service that allows you to work together with a professional artist to bring your ideas to life.
I will create a digital or physical product that you can use in your game, stream, or any other media privately or commercially.

Each Commission is unic and my take longer or shorter depending on addons, how busy I am, and other factors
A estimated time for the commission will be given in the checkout page. You can also check the status of your commission in the profile page.
If the Commission takes longer than the estimated time you are entitled 1 month to cancel your order and recive a 100% refund ...

The price is calculated as a sum of the materials (software, textures, sounds etc. for digital commissions), the selected addons, and a base Price.
The Base price a estimate of how much ours I will spend on the commission times my hourly rate of 15€.
price is them converted to your currency if necessary and complemented if you choose to have any extra revisions besides the ones given with the base product.

Unless you explicitly ask for a copyright assignment during the commission processes, I, as creator of the art work, still hold all rights.
< You still are the owner of the physical object itself. (the actual canvas, print, model files tc.) You have the right to possess it, display it and sell or give away that specific object to someone else
What you cannot do is reproduce the artwork, distribute copies, or create derivative works. Meaning, for example, to sell merchandise based on a product you purchased from me without the commercial license.

Before the finished product is sent to you (or have the watermark removed in case of a digital product) I will ask for your approval and last-minute revisions.
Deposits are non-refundable, and artworks that you have approved are also unrefundable.
